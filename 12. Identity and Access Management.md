### Identity Systems & Security  Design
**LDAP**  
- Directory based
- Hierarchically organised
    - OU – Orgosational Uniy
    - CN – Common Name
    - DC – Domain Controler
- Plain text by default, cane enable TLS
- Has 3 modes  
  - Anonymous
  - Unauthenticated
  - Username/Password
